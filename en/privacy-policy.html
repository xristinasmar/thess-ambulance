Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Thess Ambulance, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as:

  • First and last name
  • Contact details (phone, email)
  • Information you submit via the contact form

3. Purpose of Processing

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your requests
  • Provide you with transportation and nursing services
  • Improve your experience on our website

4. Data Protection

We take all necessary technical and organizational measures for the security of your data and to prevent unauthorized access or leakage.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. You can contact us for any questions.
